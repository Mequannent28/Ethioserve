4 Booking Submitted with Status "Pending"

After customer clicks Pay & Book Ticket, the system processes the booking.

  • Booking is saved to database with unique reference (e.g., BUS-A7F2E1D8)
  • Status is set to "pending" (awaiting transport owner approval)
  • Payment status is marked as "paid"
  • All passenger details, pickup/dropoff points are saved
  • Customer is redirected to confirmation page
  • Transport owner is notified of new booking

5 Transport Owner Approves & Assigns Seats

The bus company owner logs in and goes to transport/bookings.php

  • View All Bookings - Filter by status (Pending, Confirmed, Cancelled)
  • Booking Details - See all passenger info, route, and payment status
  • Assign Seat Numbers - Enter seat/cher numbers (e.g., "12, 13")
  • Approve Button - Click "Approve & Assign Seats"
  • Status Update - Booking changes from "pending" to "confirmed"
  • Customer Notification - Customer can see assigned seats
